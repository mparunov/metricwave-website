DISCOVER · ANALYSE · OPTIMISE

Process Mining & Analytics

See your operations as data — and find exactly where time and money leak out.

Every business thinks it knows how its processes work. Process mining shows you how they actually work — and the gap is almost always bigger than expected.

We extract event logs from your systems (ERP, CRM, ticketing tools, logistics platforms), build a data-driven process map, and surface the bottlenecks, deviations and rework loops that are costing you throughput and margin. You get a clear, prioritised picture of where to act — backed by data, not anecdote.

This service is especially powerful before a process redesign, automation initiative, or system migration — because it tells you what's actually happening before you decide what to change.

MANUFACTURING · P2P PROCESS

Procurement Process Discovery

Analysed purchase-to-pay event logs for a mid-size manufacturer. Discovered 3 high-frequency process variants — including an approval bypass route — that were adding an average of 18 days to the procurement cycle.

18-day cycle time reduction identified

LOGISTICS · TRANSPORT

Delivery Route & Fulfilment Analysis

Mapped end-to-end delivery operations from order creation to customer sign-off. Identified rework loops in the re-delivery process and a consistent delay pattern tied to a single depot.

31% cycle time improvement after depot fix

TECHNOLOGY · HR

Employee Onboarding Process Audit

Mapped onboarding workflows across 6 departments using Jira and HR system logs. Found that IT provisioning was the single bottleneck adding an average of 4 business days to every new hire start.

4-day bottleneck removed — onboarding SLA met

How It Works

Our delivery process for this service

Step 1

Extract Event Logs

We work with you to identify and extract the right event data from your systems — ERP, CRM, ticketing, or custom logs.

Step 2

Discover & Map

We run process discovery algorithms to build an actual map of how your process runs — all variants, frequencies and paths.

Step 3

Analyse & Diagnose

We identify bottlenecks, deviations, conformance gaps and rework patterns, with root-cause analysis for each finding.

Step 4

Recommend & Track

We deliver a prioritised improvement roadmap and, optionally, build KPI dashboards to track operational performance ongoing.
