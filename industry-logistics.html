Logistics & Mobility

Turning operational data into faster routes, leaner costs, and better customer outcomes — for operators who move things and platforms being built from scratch.

What We've Seen in This Sector

Moving companies and mobility platforms generate enormous amounts of operational data — job records, route logs, customer interactions, fleet utilization, pricing history. Most of it sits unused. The gap isn't data collection; it's the analytical infrastructure to make that data legible and actionable.

We've worked with two ends of the spectrum: established regional movers looking to tighten operational efficiency and understand their customer base more precisely, and early-stage Georgian mobility companies designing their data architecture before their first production deployment.

In both cases, the underlying problem is the same — the business is generating signals it isn't reading. We close that gap.
