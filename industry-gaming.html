Regulated SaaS & iGaming

Data operations and product delivery in environments where compliance sign-off, multi-jurisdiction constraints, and data governance are built into every workflow — not bolted on after.

What We've Seen in This Sector

Regulated iGaming is one of the most demanding operational environments there is. Product releases require compliance sign-off across multiple jurisdictions. Data flows cross regulatory boundaries. Audit trails must be maintained. And the pace — multiple major releases per quarter, across 15+ parallel product lines — leaves no margin for ambiguous process ownership or unclear data standards.

We have worked inside this environment, not advised from outside it. As part of Flutter International's product function — operating through Singular — we managed data operations and release coordination for a global SaaS platform. Over four years, that role expanded four times: from data support into business analysis, requirements gathering, and cross-functional delivery ownership.

That trajectory reflects the nature of the work. In a regulated SaaS context, the people who understand both the compliance constraints and the data infrastructure are rare. We bring that combination.

15+ Parallel SaaS applications managed within one multi-jurisdiction environment
