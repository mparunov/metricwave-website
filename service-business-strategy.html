PLANNING · OKRs · ADVISORY

Business Strategy & Management

Clear direction, grounded in data — for founders and leadership teams who want to make better decisions faster.

Good strategy without data is guesswork. Data without strategic context is noise. We bring both together — structured thinking, financial rigour, and analytics — to give your leadership team a clearer path forward.

We work with founders and senior leaders on the decisions that matter: where to invest, what to stop doing, how to set meaningful targets, and how to know if you're on track. We build the frameworks, models, and reporting structures that make those decisions faster and better — without the overhead of a full-time strategy function.

Engagements range from a single strategic planning workshop to an ongoing advisory relationship — whatever level of support fits your stage and pace.

How It Works

Our delivery process for this service

Step 1

Discovery & Context

We get deep into your business — where you are, where you want to go, what decisions are on the table, and what information you currently have (or lack).

Step 2

Analysis & Modelling

We build the analytical foundation — financial models, scenario analyses, market assessments, or performance reviews — depending on what the decision requires.

Step 3

Strategy & Frameworks

We turn analysis into clear strategic recommendations and practical frameworks your team can actually use: OKRs, roadmaps, prioritisation matrices.

Step 4

Reporting & Ongoing Support

We set up management reporting to track progress against plan, and remain available for follow-on questions, model updates, and periodic reviews.
